Justin Yates

Full Stack Web Developer

I am Justin Yates, born in Sacremento, California and currently residing in Overland Park, Kansas. I am on a journey to become a software engineer. Somewhere along the path of my Batchelors of Business Science degree I got tired of the same old routine stuff and decided to challenge myself to something I had an interest in, but never really persued. Here I am now...

It started when I built my first computer. I had never really had a computer, save a Chromebook for school, so I never realized how much I enjoyed computer science. After that I dove headfirst into everything computer. Hardware, programming, binary, you name it. I decided to take a semester off of business school and try out a Java class...

Now, I find myself enrolled in a fast paced bootcamp at the University of Kansas, honing my skills for my newfound career. So-far I find backend development and software engineering far more alluring than website design. Front-end is a little too close to the marketing path I was following beforehand.
